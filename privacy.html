Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Overview

Jpeezy TikTok Content respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account, we may access:

  • Your TikTok username and public profile information
  • Videos you choose to upload or schedule through the Service
  • Basic analytics data related to your TikTok content

We do not collect your TikTok password. Authentication is handled through TikTok's official Login Kit (OAuth).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use accessed information solely to:

  • Post videos to your TikTok account on your behalf
  • Display your profile and content within the app dashboard
  • Provide analytics and content research features

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties.

4. Data Storage

We store only the minimum data necessary to operate the Service. Video files are processed temporarily and are not retained after posting. We do not store TikTok credentials.

5. Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with TikTok's API. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy.

6. Your Rights

You may disconnect your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's app settings under "Manage app permissions." This immediately revokes our access.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at github.com/Jpeezy02.
